About TextureX

Our Story

TextureX was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate Minecraft players who wanted to enhance their gaming experience with high-quality texture packs. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a community-driven platform with thousands of users worldwide.

Today, we're proud to offer one of the largest collections of Minecraft texture packs, with options for every style and preference.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the Minecraft community with exceptional texture packs that transform the gaming experience. We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality resources, which is why all our texture packs are free to download.

We support ourselves through YouTube partnerships and donations from our amazing community.
